SafeDiffuser

Neural ODEs are continuous models that excel in learning representations from data with temporal properties, such as modeling system dynamics and controllers. Ensuring the output of neural ODEs to satisfy a certain performance (such as safety) is necessary for a variety of settings, which is defined as the forward invariance of neural ODEs. We propose a new method to ensure neural ODEs with invariance properties using Control Barrier Functions (CBFs).


What are CBFs? CBFs transform nonlinear optimizations to convex optimizations with provable guarantees.
